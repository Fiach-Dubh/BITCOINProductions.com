SATOSHI.movie

SATOSHI is our long term film project focused on bringing the character of Satoshi Nakamoto to life to inspire and educate the broader public on Bitcoin. We hope to do this in a way that honours his legacy and respects his privacy. For now we are bringing his voice to life by converting his written online conversations to an audiobook format. Snippets of this can be viewed on BITCOIN YouTube.
